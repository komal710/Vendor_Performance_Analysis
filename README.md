# Vendor_Performance_Analysis
Business Problem

In the retail and wholesale industry, effective inventory and vendor management play a crucial role in maintaining profitability and operational efficiency. Organizations often face challenges such as inefficient pricing strategies, slow inventory turnover, and over-dependence on specific vendors, which can negatively impact revenue and increase operational costs.

To address these challenges, this analysis aims to evaluate vendor and product performance using sales and inventory data. The objective is to identify key factors influencing profitability and provide data-driven insights that can support better procurement, pricing, and inventory management decisions.

Key Objectives

Identify underperforming brands and products that may require pricing adjustments or promotional strategies.

Determine top-performing vendors contributing the most to overall sales and gross profit.

Analyze the impact of bulk purchasing on unit costs to understand potential cost advantages.

Evaluate inventory turnover trends to reduce holding costs and improve operational efficiency.

Compare profitability differences between high-performing and low-performing vendors to support strategic vendor management.
